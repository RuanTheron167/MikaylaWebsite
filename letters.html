24 June 2026

Be My Girlfriend

I've been spinning my wheels over how to ask you this question ever since our holiday in Sedgefield. That's more than two months... but who's counting, right? 😉

Then one Wednesday morning, I woke up and decided, today is the day.

I wrote you my very first letter, tucked behind one of our photos, and drove to your house. While I was in the shower, I asked you to read it.

Afterwards, I asked you to be my girlfriend.

Then you looked at me and asked, "Is that it?"

Then you smiled... and you said yes.

That "yes" is the greatest one I've ever received. I don't think you'll ever fully understand how much it meant to me in that moment.

I am incredibly proud, unbelievably grateful, and so deeply thankful that I get to call you my girlfriend.

I wouldn't trade that simple little moment on your couch for the grandest proposal in the world.
